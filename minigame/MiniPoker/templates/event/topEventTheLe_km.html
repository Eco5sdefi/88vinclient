Rules:

During the event period, customers participating in the Minipoker minigame play with {Config.MoneyText [1]} on the game portal {Config.PortalName} will randomly receive the Golden Lottery symbol in one turn (session).

Each time, the system will randomly issue 1 ticket (from 00000 to 99999) from 18:00 the previous day to 18:00 the next day (Northern Lottery result opening date). For tickets issued after 18:00, the system will record for the next day.

The more Gold Tickets you accumulate, the better your chances of winning the valuable {Config.MoneyText [1]} prizes.

With a Golden Ticket received the player has a chance to win 1 jackpot worth 2,000,000 {Config.MoneyText[1]} and 9 prizes {Config.MoneyText[1]} each prize worth 200,000 {Config.MoneyText[1]}

  1. Special prize: a prize with a 5-digit ticket coinciding with the special prize of the Northern lottery opening prize at 18:00 every day.
  2. Consolation prizes: 9 consolation prizes with discrepancies in units and the special prize in the same day (coinciding with thousands, thousands, hundreds and tens).

In addition, to encourage players to earn the most gold tickets every day, they will receive a prize of 500,000 {Config.MoneyText[1]} based on the criteria for the largest number of tickets and the earliest time.
